Levi Jackson Rag (Pat Shaw)

5-cpl set, top cpl facing down (improper), other 4 cpls becket

A1. ( 8) Sides rt & lf thru while 1s down center past 2 cpls
( 8) Sides rt & lf back while 1s down center to bottom
A2. ( 8) 1s separate, come up outside to top, while sides cir lf
( 8) All P dsd
B1. (16) W grand chain (2x) ["leave 1, pass 1, turn 1"]
B2. (16) New P promenade 1 place, bal & sw
  • In A1, top couple waits for the sides to pass thru, then progresses during the courtesy turn.
  • In the grand chain the women leave their gent, pass another gent, and are courtesy turned by the next gent. When this dance breaks down, it's generally because the grand chain isn't danced snappily enough.
